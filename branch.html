AI-Enhanced Development Workflow

A typical development cycle that incorporates AI tools while maintaining transparency and human oversight.

1

Planning & AI Analysis

Use AI tools to analyze requirements, estimate complexity, and suggest implementation approaches. Team reviews and validates all AI recommendations through transparent discussion.

2

AI-Assisted Development

Developers use AI tools for code generation, documentation, and testing while maintaining clear attribution and review processes for all AI-generated content.

3

Collaborative Review

AI performs initial code analysis and quality checks, then human reviewers provide final validation and approval with full visibility into the AI's assessment criteria.

4

Deployment & Monitoring

Automated deployment with AI-powered monitoring and alerting. All decisions and metrics are transparent to the team with clear escalation paths for human intervention.
